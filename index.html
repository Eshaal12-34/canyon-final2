Faculties

Abasyn University offers diverse programs through four faculties. Each faculty is dedicated to academic excellence and career-focused education.

Faculty of Management and Social Sciences

Home to programs in Business, English, GPP, and International Relations, this faculty develops leadership and communication skills to shape future professionals in business and social sectors.

Faculty of Life Sciences

Offering programs like DPT, MLT, Radiology, Dentistry, Anesthesia, and Microbiology, this faculty trains students for impactful careers in healthcare and life sciences.

Faculty of Engineering and Computing

This faculty offers bachelor’s and master’s programs in Computer Science, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. With modern labs and a focus on practical learning, it prepares students for careers in technology and engineering.

Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences

Focused on Pharm-D, MPhil, and PhD programs, this faculty prepares students for professional and research roles in pharmacy and pharmaceutical sciences.
